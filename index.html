Hi! I'm István Varga. I’m experimenting with gameplay loops built around natural conversation. My goal is to create short, clever, and unusual narrative games - where you talk, argue, and influence the world using words alone.

I'm using LLMs, simple 2D tools, and open-source software to create unusual prototypes that will (hopefully) stick in your mind longer than a leaderboard screen.
